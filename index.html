I began on a team assigned to external facing applications and eventually joined a new team where I worked on internal applications. Between these teams, I had the opportunity to work on projects written in JavaScript and C#. Some interesting technologies utilized by these projects include Node.js, AngularJS, ASP.NET, ServiceStack, and several others.
